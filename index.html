About the Game

Fieldvalley: Seasons of the Hollow focuses on rebuilding both a place and a routine.

  • RELAX

  • GROW

  • RESTORE

Players arrive in a forgotten valley where time seems to move differently. By planting crops, repairing structures, and interacting with the environment, the homestead gradually becomes livable again.

Here's what really sets this apart - everything revolves around the seasons. Spring introduces renewal, summer rewards effort, autumn brings harvest, and winter encourages reflection and preparation. The valley never rushes the player — it simply responds to consistent care.

Life in the Valley

Discover the meaningful beauty in daily routines and the quiet wonder of seasonal changes.

  • Daily Activities

    Every day brings those little tasks that somehow feel important - you know, the kind where you look back and think 'wow, look how far I've come.' Players may plant seeds, water crops, gather materials, or explore nearby areas for hidden resources. None of these actions feel urgent; instead, they build a gentle rhythm that mirrors real rural life.

  • Seasonal Changes

    The valley basically becomes a completely different place as the year goes on. Fields change color, weather shifts, and new opportunities appear depending on the season. Some crops only grow during specific periods, while certain areas become accessible only when conditions are right.

Exploration & Discovery

Beyond farming, the valley hides quiet secrets. Old pathways lead to forgotten orchards, abandoned mills, and natural landmarks that hint at the region’s past. Don't worry about monsters or anything scary - this is all about that 'what's over that hill?' kind of wandering.

Discoveries may unlock new tools, decorative elements, or story fragments that deepen the player’s connection to the world.
